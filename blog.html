2023-12-23: New Toys

Good news! I'm finally shooting with a "real" camera again, and I'm loving it. I picked up an Oly EM10-ii and it makes my photography process incredibly easy. I've paired this camera with a 28 mm f2.8 and a 50mm f1.4, which are 56 and 100mm on full frame, respectively. Going back to what I said earlier, the live zoom, focus peaking, and flip up screen have really helped me compose my shots better and I think that demonstrates itself in my photography. For example, I've taken a lot more shots that I'm happy with recently. I will be uploading those to the gallery soon! Thanks for reading.
