urban

this is the style of photography i most identify with. the stories behind the weathered walls of buildings are interesting to me, there is a certain beauty in surrounding ourselves with the grime behind everyday life. although it may not look especially appealing to our eyes at first glance, we should appreciate the history that they have given us, and the future that they hold. this type of photography is very "life imitates art" and that is what i attempt to express in these photos.
