Cameras

My Gear

(Fujica AX-3) / Though I haven't shot photos with this camera much, I still really like the robust feel and nice technology. I have replaced the film seals and it works great! I definitely look forward to shooting it more in the future.

(Canon SD360) / I hardly ever go out without this camera. The limitations that it comes with (such as low MP count, limited zoom, and slower shutter) have pushed me to rely on less safety nets like zoom or the idea that I can just crop anything in post. In short, it's a great camera and I think carrying it around has helped me improve as a photographer.

(Nikon D3400) / This is my first "real" camera. It's a bit chunky, so I don't carry it around everywhere, but I believe it was a great first camera to have. Most of my work has come from the lenses of this camera and they turned out great. It gives you lots of options on a budget.
