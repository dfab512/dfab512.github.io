Cameras

My Gear

(Fujica AX-3) / I haven't shot photos with this camera much, but I enjoy the robust feel and nice technology. The film seals have been replaced and it works great! I look forward to using it more in the future.

(Canon SD630) / This camera hardly leaves my pockets. Its limitations have pushed me to rely on fewer safety nets like zoom or the idea that I can just crop anything in post. It's a great camera and I think carrying it around has helped me improve as a photographer.

(Olympus OM-D Em10-ii) / In the later half of 2023, I bought this camera, along with a Minolta MD adapter. So far I've only been using this camera with adapted manual focus lenses but I'm really happy with how the pictures come out. Right now, I'm planning to buy a wider auto focus lens. I hope to experiment with the 35mm focal length soon on this camera.
