Cameras

My Gear

(Fujica AX-3) / I haven't shot photos with this camera much, but I enjoy the robust feel and nice technology. The film seals have been replaced and it works great! I look forward to using it more in the future.

(Canon SD360) / This camera hardly leaves my pockets. Its limitations have pushed me to rely on fewer safety nets like zoom or the idea that I can just crop anything in post. It's a great camera and I think carrying it around has helped me improve as a photographer.

(Nikon D3400) / This is my first "real" camera. Due to its size, I don't take it with me everywhere, but I believe it was a great first camera to have. Even for its age, it's alright!
